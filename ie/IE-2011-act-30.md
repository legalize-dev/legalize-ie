# Access to Central Treasury Funds (Commission for Energy Regulation) Act  2011

##### 1. Amendment of section 18 of National Treasury Management Agency (Amendment) Act 2000.

**1**.— Section 18 of the National Treasury Management Agency (Amendment) Act 2000 is amended, in the definition of “designated body”, by deleting “and” in paragraph (*ee*), by substituting “plc,” for “plc.” in paragraph (*ef*) and by inserting after paragraph (*ef*) the following:

“(*eg*) the Commission for Energy Regulation,”.

##### 2. Citation.

**2**.— This Act may be cited as the Access to Central Treasury Funds (Commission for Energy Regulation) Act 2011.
