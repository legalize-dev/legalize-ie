# Civic Gard (Acquisition of Premises) (Amendment) Act , 1924

##### 1 Meaning of Principal Act.

**1.**—In this Act the expression “the Principal Act” means the Civic Guard (Acquisition of Premises) Act, 1923 (No. 24 of 1923).

##### 2 Duration of Principal Act.

**2.**—Section 4 of the Principal Act is hereby repealed, and in lieu thereof it is hereby enacted that the power of acquiring premises and the powers ancillary thereto conferred on the Commissioners of Public Works in Ireland by the Principal Act shall, subject to the other provisions of the Principal Act, be exercisable until the 16th day of July, 1925, and no longer.

##### 3 Short title and construction.

**3.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the Civic Guard (Acquisition of Premises) (Amendment) Act, 1924.

(2) This Act shall be construed as one with the Principal Act, and this Act and the Principal Act may be cited together as the Civic Guard (Acquisition of Premises) Acts, 1923 and 1924.
