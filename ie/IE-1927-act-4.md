# Constitution (Amendment No. 3) Act , 1927

##### 1 Cesser of holiday for general election.

**1.**—The Constitution shall be and is hereby amended by the deletion from Article 28 thereof of the words “and shall be proclaimed a public holiday.”

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Constitution (Amendment No. 3) Act 1927.
