# Rent Restrictions (Temporary Provisions)(Continuance) Act , 1981

##### 1 Duration of Rent Restrictions (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1981.

**1.**—The following section shall be substituted for section 2 of the Rent Restrictions (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1981:

“2. This Act shall have effect until the 25th day of April, 1982, and shall then expire.”.

##### 2 Short title and collective citation.

**2.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the Rent Restrictions (Temporary Provisions) (Continuance) Act, 1981.

(2) The collective citation, the Rent Restrictions Acts, 1960 to 1981, shall include this Act.
