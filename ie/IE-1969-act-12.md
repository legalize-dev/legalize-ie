# Electricity (Special Provisions)(Repeal) Act , 1969

##### 1 Repeal.

**1.**—The Electricity (Special Provisions) Act, 1966, is hereby repealed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Electricity (Special Provisions) (Repeal) Act, 1969.
