# Vocational Education (Amendment) Act  2001

##### 1 Amendment of Article 29 of the Constitution.

**1.**—Article 29 of the Constitution is hereby amended as follows:

(*a*) the section the text of which is set out in *Part 1* of the *Schedule* to this Act shall be inserted after section 8 of the Irish text,

(*b*) the section the text of which is set out in *Part 2* of the *Schedule* to this Act shall be inserted after section 8 of the English text.

##### 2 Citation.

**2.**—(1) The amendment of the Constitution effected by this Act shall be called the Twenty-third Amendment of the Constitution.

(2) This Act may be cited as the Twenty-third Amendment of the Constitution Act, 2001.

## SCHEDULE

## Part 1

9 Tig leis an Stát Reacht na Róimhe den Chúirt Choiriúil Idirnáis-iúnta, a rinneadh sa Róimh an 17ú lá d'Iúil, 1998, a dhaingniú.

## Part 2

9 The State may ratify the Rome Statute of the International Criminal Court done at Rome on the 17th day of July, 1998.
