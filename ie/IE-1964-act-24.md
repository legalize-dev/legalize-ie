# Mac Swiney (Pension) (Increase) Act , 1964

##### 1 Increase of pension.

**1.**—The annual pension payable under the MacSwiney (Pension) Acts, 1950 to 1962, shall, with effect from (and inclusive of) the 1st day of November, 1963, be increased by five per cent.

##### 2 Short title and collective citation.

**2.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the MacSwiney (Pension) (Increase) Act, 1964.

(2) The MacSwiney (Pension) Acts, 1950 to 1962, and this Act may be cited together as the MacSwiney (Pension) Acts, 1950 to 1964.
