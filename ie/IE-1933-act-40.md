# Constitution (Amendment No. 20) Act , 1933

##### 1 Amendment of Article 37 of the Constitution.

**1.**—Article 37 of the Constitution shall be and is hereby amended by the deletion of all words from the word “Representative” to the end of the Article, and the insertion, in lieu of the words so deleted, of the words “Executive Council signed by the President of the Executive Council.”

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Constitution (Amendment No. 20) Act, 1933.
