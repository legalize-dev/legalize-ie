# Chester Beatty Library Act , 1968

##### 1 Power of President to appoint library trustee.

**1.**—Subject to the terms and conditions contained in the will of Sir Alfred Chester Beatty, deceased, the President of Ireland may from time to time appoint a person to be a library trustee for the purposes of the said will and the codicil thereto dated the 17th day of November, 1966.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Chester Beatty Library Act, 1968.
