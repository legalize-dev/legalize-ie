##### 1. **Interpretation.**

1.—(1)In this Act “the Act of 1952” means the Tourist Traffic Act, 1952.

(2)The Tourist Traffic Acts, 1939 to 1963, and this Act shall be construed as one.

##### 2. **Caravan sites and camping sites.**
