# School Attendance Act , 1936

##### 1 The Principal Act.

**1.**—In this Act the expression “the Principal Act” means the School Attendance Act, 1926 (No. 17 of 1926).

##### 2 Amendment of sub-sections (3) and (4) of section 4 of Principal Act.

**2.**—(1) Sub-sections (3) and (4) of section 4 of the Principal Act are hereby amended by the substitution in each of those sub-sections of the words “the year 1940” for the words “the year 1936.”

(2) This section shall have and be deemed to have had effect as from the commencement of the said section 4.

##### 3 Short title and citation.

**3.**—This Act may be cited as the School Attendance Act, 1936, and the Principal Act and this Act may be cited together as the School Attendance Acts, 1926 and 1936.
