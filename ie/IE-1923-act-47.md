# Expiring Laws Act , 1923

##### 1 Acts in First Schedule to become permanent.

**1.**—(1) The Acts mentioned in the First Schedule to this Act shall, to the extent specified in the third column of that schedule, become permanent Acts, and any provision in any Act in force at the date of the passing of this Act which limits the period for which any of those Acts or any parts thereof are to remain in operation shall cease to have effect.

(2) Any unrepealed enactments amending or affecting the enactments made permanent by this section shall, in so far as they are temporary in their duration, become permanent in like manner, whether they are mentioned in the First Schedule to this Act or not.

##### 2 Acts in Second Schedule to be continued.

**2.**—(1) The Acts mentioned in the Second Schedule to this Act shall, to the extent specified in the third column of that schedule, be continued until the 31st day of December, 1924, and shall then expire unless further continued.

(2) Any unrepealed enactments amending or affecting the enactments continued by this section shall, in so far as they are temporary in their duration, be continued in like manner, whether they are mentioned in the Second Schedule to this Act or not.

##### 3 Short title.

**3.**—This Act may be cited as the Expiring Laws Act, 1923.
