# Statute Law Revision (Pre-1922) Act  2005

##### 1 Repeals.

**1.**—The statutes of Ireland, England, Great Britain and the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland mentioned in the *Schedule* to this Act are repealed.

##### 2 Saving.

**2.**—(1) This Act does not affect any existing principle or rule of law or equity, or any established jurisdiction, form or course of pleading, practice or procedure, notwithstanding that it may have been in any manner derived from, affirmed or recognised by any enactment repealed by this Act.

(2) Where any enactment not repealed by this Act has been confirmed, revived or perpetuated by an enactment repealed by this Act, such confirmation, revival or perpetuation is not affected by the repeal.

##### 3 Short title.

**3.**—This Act may be cited as the Statute Law Revision (Pre-1922) Act 2005.
