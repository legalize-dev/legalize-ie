# Trade Union Act , 1935

##### 1 Amendment of section 7 of the Trade Union Act, 1871.

**1.**—Section 7 of the Trade Union Act, 1871, shall be construed and have effect as if the words “not exceeding one acre” now contained therein were deleted therefrom.

##### 2 Short title and Citation.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Trade Union Act, 1935, and the Trade Union Acts, 1871 to 1917, and this Act may be cited together as the Trade Union Acts, 1871 to 1935.
