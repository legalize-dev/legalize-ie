# Restrictive Trade Practices (Confirmation of Orders)(No. 3) Act , 1956

##### 1 Confirmation of Order.

**1.**—The Restrictive Trade Practices (Motor Cars) Order, 1956 (S.I. No. 86 of 1956), made by the Minister for Industry and Commerce under section 9 of the Restrictive Trade Practices Act, 1953 (No. 14 of 1953), is hereby confirmed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Restrictive Trade Practices (Confirmation of Order) (No. 3) Act, 1956.
