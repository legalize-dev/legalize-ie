# Agriculture (Amendment) Act , 1967

##### 1 Repeal of section 2 (1) (*a*) of Agriculture (Amendment) Act, 1964.

**1.**—Section 2 (1) (*a*) of the Agriculture (Amendment) Act, 1964, is hereby repealed.

##### 2 Short title and collective citation.

**2.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the Agriculture (Amendment) Act, 1967.

(2) The Agriculture Acts, 1931 to 1964, and this Act may be cited together as the Agriculture Acts, 1931 to 1967.
