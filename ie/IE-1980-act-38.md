# Restrictive Practices (Confirmation of Order)(No. 2) Act , 1980

##### 1 Confirmation of Order.

**1.**—The Restrictive Practices (Motor Spirit) (No. 2) Order, 1980 (S.I. No. 376 of 1980), made by the Minister for Industry, Commerce and Tourism under section 8 of the Restrictive Practices Act, 1972, is hereby confirmed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Restrictive Practices (Confirmation of Order) (No. 2) Act, 1980.
