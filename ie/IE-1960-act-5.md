# Pensions (Amendment) Act , 1960

##### 1 Increase of pensions.

**1.**—The annual pensions payable under section 2 and section 3 of the Pensions Act, 1952, shall, with effect from the 1st day of August, 1959, be increased from five hundred pounds to five hundred and sixty-one pounds and sixteen shillings.

##### 2 Short title and collective citation.

**2.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the Pensions (Amendment) Act, 1960.

(2) The Pensions Act, 1952, and this Act may be cited together as the Pensions Acts, 1952 and 1960.
