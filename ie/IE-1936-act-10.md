# Imposition of Duties (Confirmation of Order) Act , 1936

##### 1 Confirmation of Order.

**1.**—The Emergency Imposition of Duties (No. 97) Order, 1936, (which relates to the reduction of the rates of certain duties of customs on articles imported from the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland) made by the Executive Council under the Emergency Imposition of Duties Act, 1932 (No. 16 of 1932), is hereby confirmed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Imposition of Duties (Confirmation of Order) Act, 1936.
