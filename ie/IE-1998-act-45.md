# Tourist Traffic Act  1998

##### 1 Interpretation.

**1.**—In this Act “the Board” means Bord Fáilte Éireann.

##### 2 Increase of certain non-repayable grants to Board.

**2.**—The aggregate amount of the sums that the Minister for Tourism, Sport and Recreation may pay to the Board for the purpose of the giving of grants by the Board for development works of the kind mentioned in section 2(1) of the Tourist Traffic Act, 1975, shall not exceed £50,000,000 and, accordingly, section 2(1) of that Act is hereby amended by the substitution of “£50,000,000” for the sum specified therein.

##### 3 Short title and collective citation.

**3.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the Tourist Traffic Act, 1998.

(2) The Tourist Traffic Acts, 1939 to 1995 and this Act may be cited together as the Tourist Traffic Acts, 1939 to 1998.
