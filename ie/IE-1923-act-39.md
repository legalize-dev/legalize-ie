# Dyestuffs (Import Regulation) Repeal Act , 1923

##### 1 Repeal of the Dyestuffs (Import Regulation) Act, 1920.

**1.**—If and so far as the Act passed by the Parliament of the late United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland on the 23rd day of December, 1920, and entitled “The Dyestuffs (Import Regulation) Act, 1920,” is in force in Saorstát Eireann, the same shall be and is hereby repealed.

##### 2 Short Title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Dyestuffs (Import Regulation) Repeal Act, 1923.
