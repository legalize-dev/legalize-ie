# Restrictive Practices (Confirmation of Order) Act  1978

##### 1 Confirmation of Order. S.I. No. 82 of 1978.

**1.**—The Restrictive Practices (Groceries) (Amendment) Order, 1978, made by the Minister for Industry, Commerce and Energy under section 8 of the Restrictive Practices Act, 1972, is hereby confirmed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Restrictive Practices (Confirmation of Order) Act, 1978.
