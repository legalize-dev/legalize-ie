# Rates on Agricultural Land (Relief)(No. 2) Act , 1951

##### 1 Application of the Rates on Agricultural Land (Relief) Act, 1946, to 1951-52.

**1.**—The Rates on Agricultural Land (Relief) Act, 1946 (No. 36 of 1946), shall apply to the local financial year ending on the 31st day of March, 1952.

##### 2 Short title and collective citation.

**2.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the Rates on Agricultural Land (Relief) Act, 1951.

(2) The Rates on Agricultural Land (Relief) Acts, 1939 to 1950, and this Act may be cited together as the Rates on Agricultural Land (Relief) Acts, 1939 to 1951.
