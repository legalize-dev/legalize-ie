# Juries (Protection) Act , 1931

##### 1 Continuance of the Juries (Protection) Act, 1929.

**1.**—The Juries (Protection) Act, 1929 (No. 33 of 1929), shall continue in force until the 30th day of September, 1933, and shall then expire.

##### 2 Short title and citation.

**2.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the Juries (Protection) Act, 1931.

(2) The Juries Acts, 1927 and 1929, and this Act may be cited together as the Juries Acts, 1927 to 1931.
