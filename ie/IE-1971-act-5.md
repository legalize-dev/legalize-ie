# Control of Exports (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1956 (Continuance) Act , 1971

##### 1 Continuance of the Control of Exports (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1956.

**1.**—(1) The Control of Exports (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1956, shall continue in force until the 31st day of March, 1974, and shall then expire.

##### 2 (2) The Control of Exports (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1956 (Continuance) Act, 1968, is hereby repealed.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Control of Exports (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1956 (Continuance) Act, 1971.
