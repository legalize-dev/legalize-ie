# European Communities (Amendment) Act , 1995

##### 1 Amendment of section 4 of European Communities Act, 1972.

**1.**—Section 4 (inserted by the European Communities (Amendment) Act, 1973) of the European Communities Act, 1972, is hereby amended by the substitution for “Joint Committee on Foreign Affairs” (inserted by section 6 of the European Communities (Amendment) Act, 1993) of “Joint Committee on European Affairs”.

##### 2 Short title, collective citation and construction.

**2.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the European Communities (Amendment) Act, 1995.

(2) The European Communities Acts, 1972 to 1994, and this Act may be cited together as the European Communities Acts, 1972 to 1995, and shall be construed together as one Act.
