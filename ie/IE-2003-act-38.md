# European Communities (Amendment) Act  2003

##### 1 Amendment of section 1 of European Communities Act 1972.

**1.**—(1) The definition of “the treaties governing the European Communities” in section 1(1) of the European Communities Act 1972 is amended by the insertion after paragraph (*t*) (inserted by the European Communities (Amendment) Act 2002) of the following:

“and

(*u*) the Treaty concerning the accession of the Czech Republic, the Republic of Estonia, the Republic of Cyprus, the Republic of Latvia, the Republic of Lithuania, the Republic of Hungary, the Republic of Malta, the Republic of Poland, the Republic of Slovenia and the Slovak Republic to the European Union done at Athens on the 16th day of April 2003, in so far as that Treaty relates to the European Communities.”.

##### 2 Short title, collective citation, construction and commencement.

**2.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the European Communities (Amendment) Act 2003.

(2) The European Communities Acts 1972 to 2002, and this Act may be cited together as the European Communities Acts 1972 to 2003, and shall be construed together as one Act.

(3) This Act shall come into operation on such day as may be appointed by order made by the Minister for Foreign Affairs.
