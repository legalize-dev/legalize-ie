# Restrictive Trade Practices (Confirmation of Orders) Act , 1972

##### 1 Confirmation of Orders.

**1.**—The Restrictive Trade Practices (Electrical Appliances and Equipment) Order, 1971, and the Restrictive Trade Practices (Electrical Appliances and Equipment) (Amendment) Order, 1971, made by the Minister for Industry and Commerce under section 9 of the Restrictive Trade Practices Act, 1953, are hereby confirmed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Restrictive Trade Practices (Confirmation of Orders) Act, 1972.
