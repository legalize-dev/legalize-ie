# Housing Finance Agency (Amendment) Act 2025

##### 1. **Amendment of section 10 of Housing Finance Agency Act 1981**

**1.** Section 10(3) of the Housing Finance Agency Act 1981 is amended by the substitution of “€13,500,000,000” for “€12,000,000,000”.

##### 2. **Short title, collective citation and construction**

**2.** (1) This Act may be cited as the Housing Finance Agency (Amendment) Act 2025.

(2) This Act and the Housing Finance Agency Acts 1981 to 2024 may be cited together as the Housing Finance Agency Acts 1981 to 2025 and shall be read together as one.
