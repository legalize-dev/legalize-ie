# League of Nations (Guarantee) Act , 1923

##### 1 Executive Council may give guarantees.

**1.**—It shall be lawful for the Executive Council of Saorstát Eireann, either as a condition precedent to the admission of Saorstát Eireann to the League of Nations or at any time after such admission, to give to the League, in the name and on behalf of Saorstát Eireann, such guarantees as shall be thought proper by the Executive Council and be acceptable to the League, of the sincere intention of Saorstát Eireann to observe its international obligations.

##### 2 Executive Council may accept regulations.

**2.**—It shall be lawful for the Executive Council of Saorstát Eireann, either as a condition precedent to the admission of Saorstát Eireann to the League of Nations or at any time after such admission, to accept in the name and on behalf of Saorstát Eireann, such regulations asar e, or subject to ratification by the Oireachtas hereafter may be prescribed by the League in regard to its military, naval and air forces and armaments.

##### 3 Short Title.

**3.**—This Act may be cited as the League of Nations (Guarantee) Act, 1923.
