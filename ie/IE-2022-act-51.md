# National Tourism Development Authority (Amendment) Act 2022

##### 1. **Amendment of section 24 of National Tourism Development Authority Act 2003**

**1.** Section 24 of the National Tourism Development Authority Act 2003 is amended, in subsection (2), by the substitution of “€500,000,000” for “€300,000,000”.

##### 2. **Short title**

**2.** This Act may be cited as the National Tourism Development Authority (Amendment) Act 2022.
