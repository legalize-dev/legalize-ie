# Control of Exports (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1956 (Continuance) Act , 1974

##### 1 Continuance of the Control of Exports (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1956.

**1.**—(1) The Control of Exports (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1956, shall continue in force until the 31st day of December, 1977, and shall then expire.

(2) The Control of Exports (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1956 (Continuance) Act, 1971, is hereby repealed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Control of Exports (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1956 (Continuance) Act, 1974.
