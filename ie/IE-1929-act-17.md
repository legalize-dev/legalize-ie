# Betting Act , 1929

##### 1 Continuance of Betting Act, 1926.

**1.**—The Betting Act, 1926 (No. 38 of 1926), shall continue in force until the 31st day of December, 1929, and shall then expire.

##### 2 Short title and citation.

**2.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the Betting Act, 1929.

(2) The Betting Acts, 1926 and 1928, and this Act may be cited together as the Betting Acts, 1926 to 1929.
