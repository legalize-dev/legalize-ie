# Consumer Protection (Regulation of Credit Servicing Firms) Act 2018

##### 1. **Amendment of Article 40 of the Constitution**

**1.** Article 40 of the Constitution is hereby amended as follows:

(a) the subsection, the text of which is set out in *Part 1* of the *Schedule*, shall be substituted for subsection 3 of section 3 of the Irish text;

(b) the subsection, the text of which is set out in *Part 2* of the *Schedule*, shall be substituted for subsection 3 of section 3 of the English text.

##### 2. **Citation**

**2.** (1) The amendment of the Constitution effected by this Act shall be called the Thirty-sixth Amendment of the Constitution.

(2) This Act may be cited as the Thirty-sixth Amendment of the Constitution Act 2018.

## **SCHEDULE**

## PART 1

3 Féadfar socrú a dhéanamh le dlí chun foirceannadh toirchis a rialáil.

## PART 2

3 Provision may be made by law for the regulation of termination of pregnancy.
