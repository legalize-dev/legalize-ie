# Social Welfare (Temporary Provisions) Act , 1981

##### 1 Increase of pensions, certain allowances and certain benefits under the Social Welfare (Consolidation) Act, 1981.

**1.**—Where a person is or becomes entitled to payment of any of the following pensions, benefits or allowances under the Social Welfare (Consolidation) Act, 1981—

(*a*) disablement pension,

(*b*) death benefit by way of pension,

(*c*) old age (contributory) pension,

(*d*) retirement pension,

(*e*) survivor's benefit under section 87,

(*f*) invalidity pension,

(*g*) deserted wife's benefit,

(*h*) widow's (contributory) pension,

(*i*) orphan's (contributory) allowance,

(*j*) old age pension,

(*k*) blind pension,

(*l*) widow's (non-contributory) pension,

(*m*) orphan's (non-contributory) pension,

(*n*) deserted wife's allowance,

(*o*) prisoner's wife's allowance,

(*p*) social assistance allowance,

(*q*) single woman's allowance,

in respect of the week commencing on—

(i) the 10th day of December, 1981, in the case of a pension, benefit or allowance mentioned at paragraph (*d*), (*f*), (*g*), (*n*), (*o*), (*p*) or (*q*) of this section, or

(ii) the 11th day of December, 1981, in the case of a pension, benefit or allowance mentioned at paragraph (*a*), (*b*), (*c*), (*e*), (*h*), (*i*), (*j*), (*k*), (*l*) or (*m*) of this section,

any amount payable to him by way of that pension, benefit or allowance (including any increases) for the appropriate week shall be increased by a sum equal to that amount.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Social Welfare (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1981.
