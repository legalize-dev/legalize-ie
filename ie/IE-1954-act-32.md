# Mortmain (Repeal of Enactments) Act , 1954

##### 1 Repeals.

**1.**—The enactments specified in the Schedule to this Act are hereby repealed to the extent mentioned in the third column of the said Schedule.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Mortmain (Repeal of Enactments) Act, 1954.
