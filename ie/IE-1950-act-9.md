# Flax Act, 1936 (Suspension) Act , 1950

##### 1 Further suspension of operation of Flax Act, 1936.

**1.**—The operation of the Flax Act, 1936 (No. 20 of 1936), and of any orders and regulations thereunder is hereby further suspended from the 1st day of January, 1950, until such day as may be fixed by order of the Government.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Flax Act, 1936 (Suspension) Act, 1950.
