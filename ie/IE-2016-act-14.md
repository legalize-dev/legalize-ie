# National Tourism Development Authority (Amendment) Act  2016

##### 1. **Grants to National Tourism Development Authority**

**1.** The National Tourism Development Authority Act 2003 is amended by substituting for section 24 (as amended by the National Tourism Development Authority (Amendment) Act 2011) the following:

“**24.** (1) The Minister may, with the consent of the Minister for Public Expenditure and Reform, advance to the Authority out of moneys provided by the Oireachtas such sums as the Minister may determine.

(2) The aggregate of moneys advanced under subsection (1) in relation to capital expenditure on projects or enterprises to which section 8(1)(e) applies shall not exceed €300,000,000.”.

##### 2. **Short title and collective citation**

**2.** (1) This Act may be cited as the National Tourism Development Authority (Amendment) Act 2016.

(2) The Tourist Traffic Acts 1939 to 2011 and this Act may be cited together as the Tourist Traffic Acts 1939 to 2016.
