# Aliens Restriction (Amendment) Act , 1931

##### 1 Repeal of certain enactments.

**1.**—The enactments mentioned in the Schedule to this Act are hereby repealed to the extent specified in the third column of the said Schedule.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Aliens Restriction (Amendment) Act, 1931.

## Schedule. *Enactments Repealed*.

| Session and Chapter. | Short Title. | Extent of Repeal. |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 8 & 9 Geo. 5. c. 31. | The Trading with the Enemy (Amendment) Act, 1918. | Section 2. |
| 9 & 10 Geo. 5 c. 92. | The Aliens Restriction (Amendment) Act, 1919. | Section 12. |
