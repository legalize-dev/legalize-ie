# Tariff Commission (Repeal) Act , 1939

##### 1 Repeal of Tariff Commission Acts, 1926 and 1930.

**1.**—The Tariff Commission Act, 1926 (No. 40 of 1926), and the Tariff Commission Act, 1930 (No. 31 of 1930), are hereby repealed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Tariff Commission (Repeal) Act, 1939.
