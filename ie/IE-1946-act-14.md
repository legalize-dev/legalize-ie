# Tourist Traffic (Amendment) Act , 1946

##### 1 Act of 1939.

**1.**—In this Act the expression “the Act of 1939” means the Tourist Traffic Act, 1939 (No. 24 of 1939).

##### 2 Amendment of subsection (1) of section 16 of the Act of 1939.

**2.**—Section 16 of the Act of 1939 is hereby amended by the substitution, for subsection (1), of the following subsection:

“(1) The Minister for Finance, on the recommendation of the Minister, may advance to the Board out of the Central Fund or the growing produce thereof such sums, not exceeding in the aggregate one million two hundred and fifty thousand pounds as the Board shall from time to time require.”.

##### 3 Short title and collective citation.

**3.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the Tourist Traffic (Amendment) Act, 1946.

(2) The Act of 1939 and this Act may be cited together as the Tourist Traffic Acts, 1939 and 1946.
