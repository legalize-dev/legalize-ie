# Court Officers Act , 1972

##### 1 Repeal of section 27 (3) of Court Officers Act, 1926.

**1.**—Section 27 (3) of the Court Officers Act, 1926, is hereby repealed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Court Officers Act, 1972.
