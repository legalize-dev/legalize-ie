# Control of Exports (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1956 (Continuance) Act , 1958

##### 1 Continuance of Control of Exports (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1956.

**1.**—(1) The Control of Exports (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1956, shall continue in force until the 31st day of March, 1962, and shall then expire.

(2) Subsection (2) of section 7 of the Control of Exports (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1956, is hereby repealed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Control of Exports (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1956 (Continuance) Act, 1958.
