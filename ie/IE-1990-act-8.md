# Horse Breeding Act , 1990

##### 1 Repeal of Horse Breeding Act, 1934.

**1.**—The Horse Breeding Act, 1934, is hereby repealed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Horse Breeding Act, 1990.
