# Electricity Regulation (Carbon Revenue Levy) (Amendment) Act  2012

##### 1. Amendment of section 40D (carbon revenue levy) of Electricity Regulation Act 1999.

**1**.— Section 40D (inserted by section 3 of the Electricity Regulation (Amendment) (Carbon Revenue Levy) Act 2010) of the Electricity Regulation Act 1999 is amended in subsection (4) by substituting “the date of the passing of the *Electricity Regulation (Carbon Revenue Levy) (Amendment) Act 2012*” for “31 December 2012”.

##### 2. Short title.

**2**.— This Act may be cited as the Electricity Regulation (Carbon Revenue Levy) (Amendment) Act 2012.
