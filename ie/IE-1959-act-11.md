# Rates on Agricultural Land (Relief) Act , 1959

##### 1 Amendment of Rates on Agricultural Land (Relief) Act, 1953.

**1.**—(1) The Rates on Agricultural Land (Relief) Act, 1953, Shall have effect in relation to the local financial years ending on the 31st day of March, 1960, the 31st day of March, 1961, and the the 31st day of March, 1962.

(2) Accordingly, the word “eight” is hereby substituted for the word “five” where the latter word occurs in section 1 of the Rates on Agricultural Land (Relief) Act, 1953, as amended by subsection (2) of section 1 of the Rates on Agricultural Land (Relief) Act, 1956, in the reference to the local financial year ending on the 31st day of March, 1954, and the five following local financial years.

##### 2 Short title and collective citation.

**2.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the Rates on Agricultural Land (Relief) Act, 1959.

(2) The Rates on Agricultural Land (Relief) Acts, 1939 to 1956, and this Act may be cited together as the Rates on Agricultural Land (Relief) Acts, 1939 to 1959.
