# Agricultural (Amendment) Act , 1970

##### 1 Amendment of section 2 (1) of the Agriculture (Amendment) Act, 1958.

**1.**—(1) Section 2 (1) of the Agriculture (Amendment) Act, 1958, as amended by section 1 of the Agriculture (Amendment) Act, 1964, is hereby amended by the substitution of “1970” and “twenty-seven” for “1965” and “twenty-one” respectively.

(2) All acts and things done before the 1st day of April, 1970, by the council of a county in relation to the making of any rate for the local financial year beginning on that day shall be as valid and effectual as if this Act had been in force at the time those acts or things were done.

##### 2 Short title, collective citation and commencement.

**2.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the Agriculture (Amendment) Act, 1970.

(2) The Agriculture Acts, 1931 to 1967, and this Act may be cited together as the Agriculture Acts, 1931 to 1970.

(3) This Act shall come into operation on the 1st day of April, 1970.
