# Housing (Gaeltacht)(Amendment) Act , 1931

##### 1 Increase of aggregate amount of grants and loans.

**1.**—The Minister for Finance may, as and when he thinks proper and subject to such conditions as he thinks fit to impose, increase by such sums, not exceeding in the whole the sum of one hundred thousand pounds, as he shall from time to time determine, the limit of two hundred and fifty thousand pounds imposed by sub-section (1) of Section 11 of the Housing (Gaeltacht) Act, 1929 (No. 41 of 1929), on the aggregate amount of the grants and loans to be made under that Act.

##### 2 Short title and citation.

**2.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the Housing (Gaeltacht) (Amendment) Act, 1931.

(2) The Housing (Gaeltacht) Act, 1929 (No. 41 of 1929), and this Act may be cited together as the Housing (Gaeltacht) Acts, 1929 and 1931.
