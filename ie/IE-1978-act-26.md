# Local Authorities (Higher Education Grants) Act , 1978

##### 1 Amendment of Local Authorities (Higher Education Grants) Act, 1968.

**1.**—(1) The Local Authorities (Higher Education Grants) Act, 1968, is hereby amended by the substitution of the following definition for the definition of “approved institution”:

“‘approved institution’ means a university, university college, or other institution of higher education in so far as it provides a course or courses of not less than two years duration, being a course or courses of which the Minister approves for the time being for the purposes of section 2 of this Act.”

(2) This section shall be deemed to have come into operation on the 1st day of August, 1974.

##### 2 Short title and collective citation.

**2.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the Local Authorities (Higher Education Grants) Act, 1978.

(2) The Local Authorities (Higher Education Grants) Act, 1968, and this Act may be cited together as the Local Authorities (Higher Education Grants) Acts, 1968 and 1978.
