# Restrictive Trade Practices (Confirmation of Order) Act , 1966

##### 1 Confirmation of Order.

**1.**—The Restrictive Trade Practices (Intoxicating Liquor and Non-alcoholic Beverages) Order, 1965, made by the Minister for Industry and Commerce under section 9 of the Restrictive Trade Practices Act, 1953, is hereby confirmed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Restrictive Trade Practices (Confirmation of Order) Act, 1966.
