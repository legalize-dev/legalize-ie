# Local Government (Repeal of Enactments) Act , 1964

##### 1 Repeals.

**1.**—The enactments mentioned in the Schedule to this Act are hereby repealed to the extent specified in the third column to that Schedule.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Local Government (Repeal of Enactments) Act, 1964.
