# Restrictive Practices (Confirmation of Orders) Act , 1974

##### 1 Confirmation of Orders.

**1.**—The Restrictive Practices (Groceries) Order, 1973, and the Restrictive Practices (Groceries) (Amendment) Order, 1973, made by the Minister for Industry and Commerce under section 8 of the, Restrictive Practices Act, 1972, are hereby confirmed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Restrictive Practices (Confirmation of Orders) Act, 1974.
