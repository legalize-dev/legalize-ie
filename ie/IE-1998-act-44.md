# State Property Act  1998

##### 1 Amendment of National Stud Act, 1945.

**1.**—The National Stud Act, 1945, is hereby amended by the repeal of section 7.

##### 2 Amendment of State Property Act, 1954.

**2.**—The State Property Act, 1954, is hereby amended in the First Schedule by the repeal of paragraph 5.

##### 3 Short title.

**3.**—This Act may be cited as the State Property Act, 1998.
