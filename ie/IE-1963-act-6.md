# Restrictive Trade Practices (Confirmation of Order) Act , 1963

##### 1 Confirmation of Order. 1962, S.I. No. 197, 1953, No. 14.

**1.**—The Restrictive Trade Practices (Hand Knitting Yarns) Order, 1962, made by the Minister for Industry and Commerce under section 9 of the Restrictive Trade Practices Act, 1953, is hereby confirmed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Restrictive Trade Practices (Confirmation of Order) Act, 1963.
