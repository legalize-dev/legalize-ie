# Social Welfare, Pensions and Civil Registration Act 2018

##### 1. **Amendment of Article 40 of the Constitution**

**1.** Article 40 of the Constitution is hereby amended as follows:

(a) “ceannairceach” shall be substituted for “diamhaslach nó ceannairceach” in paragraph i of subsection 1 of section 6 of the Irish text;

(b) “seditious” shall be substituted for “blasphemous, seditious,” in paragraph i of subsection 1 of section 6 of the English text.

##### 2. **Citation**

**2.** (1) The amendment of the Constitution effected by this Act shall be called the Thirty-seventh Amendment of the Constitution.

(2) This Act may be cited as the Thirty-seventh Amendment of the Constitution (Repeal of offence of publication or utterance of blasphemous matter) Act 2018.
