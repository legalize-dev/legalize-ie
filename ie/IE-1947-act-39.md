# Supplies and Services (Temporary Provisions) Bill 1946 (Continuance) Act  1947

##### 1 The Principal Act.

**1.**—In this Act, the expression “the Principal Act” means the Supplies and Services (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1946 (No. 22 of 1946).

##### 2 Continuance of the Principal Act.

**2.**—(1) The Principal Act shall, unless previously terminated under subsection (2) of this section, continue in force until the 31st day of December, 1948, and shall then expire unless the Oireachtas otherwise determines.

(2) The Government may by order declare that the Principal Act shall expire on a specified date, being earlier than the 31st day of December, 1948, and in that case the Principal Act shall expire accordingly.

(3) Subsections (3) and (4) of section 16 of the Principal Act are hereby repealed.

##### 3 Short title.

**3.**—This Act may be cited as the Supplies and Services (Temporary Provisions) Act, 1946 (Continuance) Act, 1947.
