# Constitution (Amendment No. 10) Act , 1928

##### 1 Amendment of Article 14 of the Constitution.

**1.**—Article 14 of the Constitution shall be and is hereby amended by the deletion of the words “and Initiative” now contained therein.

##### 2 Repeal of Article 47 of the Constitution.

**2.**—The Constitution shall be and is hereby amended by the deletion of Article 47 thereof.

##### 3 Repeal of Article 48 of the Constitution.

**3.**—The Constitution shall be and is hereby amended by the deletion of Article 48 thereof.

##### 4 Amendment of Article 50 of the Constitution.

**4.**—Article 50 of the Constitution shall be and is hereby amended by the deletion of the words “and as such shall be subject to the provisions of Article 47 hereof” now contained therein.

##### 5 Short title.

**5.**—This Act may be cited as the Constitution (Amendment No. 10) Act, 1928.
