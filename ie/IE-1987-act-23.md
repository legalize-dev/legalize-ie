# Restrictive Practices (Confirmation of Order) Act , 1987

##### 1 Confirmation of Order.

**1.**—The Restrictive Practices (Groceries) Order, 1987, made by the Minister for Industry and Commerce under section 8 of the Restrictive Practices Act, 1972, is hereby confirmed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Restrictive Practices (Confirmation of Order) Act, 1987.
