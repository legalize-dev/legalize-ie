# Constitution (Amendment No. 26) Act , 1935

##### 1 Amendment of Article 3 of the Constitution.

**1.**—Article 3 of the Constitution is hereby amended in the following respects and shall be construed and have effect accordingly, that is to say:—

(*a*) by the deletion from the said Article of the words “within the limits of the jurisdiction of the Irish Free State (Saorstát Eireann)”, and

(*b*) by the deletion from the proviso to the said Article of the word “in” and the substitution for the word so deleted of the word “of.”

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Constitution (Amendment No. 26) Act, 1935.
