# Restrictive Trade Practices (Confirmation of Orders)(No. 2) Act , 1962

##### 1 Confirmation of Order.

**1.**—The Restrictive Trade Practices (Cookers and Ranges) Order, 1962, made by the Minister for Industry and Commerce under section 9 of the Restrictive Trade Practices Act, 1953, is hereby confirmed.

##### 2 Short title.

**2.**—This Act may be cited as the Restrictive Trade Practices (Confirmation of Order) (No. 2) Act, 1962.
