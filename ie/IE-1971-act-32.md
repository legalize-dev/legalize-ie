# Agriculture (Amendment) Act , 1971

##### 1 Amendment of section 2 of the Agriculture (Amendment) Act, 1958.

**1.**—(1) Section 2 of the Agriculture (Amendment) Act, 1958, as amended by section 1 of the Agriculture (Amendment) Act, 1964, and section 1 of the Agriculture (Amendment) Act, 1970, is hereby further amended by the substitution for subsection (1) of the following subsection:

“(1) The council of a county shall, in the local financial year beginning on the 1st day of April, 1972, and every subsequent local financial year, make to the committee of agriculture for the county a contribution under this subsection of not less than the produce of a rate in the preceding local financial year of one new penny in the pound in the area consisting of the county exclusive of every (if any) urban district therein and not more than fifteen times such produce.”.

(2) All acts and things done before the 1st day of April, 1972, by the council of a county in relation to the making of any rate for the local financial year beginning on that day shall be as valid and effectual as if this Act had been in force at the time those acts or things were done.

##### 2 Short title, collective citation and commencement.

**2.**—(1) This Act may be cited as the Agriculture (Amendment) Act, 1971.

(2) The Agriculture Acts, 1931 to 1970, and this Act may be cited together as the Agriculture Acts, 1931 to 1971.

(3) This Act shall come into operation on the 1st day of April, 1972.
