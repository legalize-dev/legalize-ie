# Constitution (Removal of Oath) Act , 1933

##### 1 Deletion of Article 17 and amendment of Article 55 of the Constitution.

**1.**—The Constitution shall be and is hereby amended as follows, that is to say:—

(*a*) by the deletion of Article 17 thereof; and

(*b*) by the deletion of the words “and shall comply with the provisions of Article 17 of this Constitution” now contained in Article 55 thereof.

##### 2 Amendment of the Constitution of the Irish Free State (Saorstát Eireann) Act, 1922.

**2.**—Section 2 of the Constitution of the Irish Free State (Saorstát Eireann) Act, 1922 (No. 1 of 1922), is hereby repealed.

##### 3 Amendment of Article 50 of the Constitution.

**3.**—Article 50 of the Constitution shall be and is hereby amended by the deletion of the words “within the terms of the Scheduled Treaty” now contained therein.

##### 4 Short title.

**4.**—This Act may be cited as the Constitution (Removal of Oath) Act, 1933.
