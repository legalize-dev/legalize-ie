# Appropriation Act , 1988

##### 1 Issue of £15,147,359 out of the Central Fund for the year 1986.

**1.**—The Minister for Finance may issue out of the Central Fund and apply towards making good the supply granted for the service of the year ended on the 31st day of December, 1986, the sum of fifteen million, one hundred and forty-seven thousand, three hundred and fifty-nine pounds.

##### 2 Appropriation of sums voted for supply services, viz., £6,453,930,359.

**2.**—(1) The sums granted by the Central Fund (Permanent Provisions) Act, 1965, and this Act out of the Central Fund towards making good the supply granted, amounting in the aggregate to the sum of six thousand, four hundred and fifty-three million, nine hundred and thirty thousand, three hundred and fifty-nine pounds and comprising the sum of fifteen million, one hundred and forty-seven thousand, three hundred and fifty-nine pounds for the service of the year ended on the 31st day of December, 1986, and the sum of six thousand, four hundred and thirty-eight million, seven hundred and eighty-three thousand pounds for the service of the year ending on the 31st day of December, 1988 are appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule annexed hereto.

(2) In addition to the grants out of the Central Fund referred to in the foregoing subsection of this section, there may be applied out of any money directed under section 2 of the Public Accounts and Charges Act, 1891, to be applied as appropriations-in-aid of the grants for the services and purposes specified in the Schedule annexed hereto, the sums respectively set forth in column (4) of the said Schedule amounting, as appears thereby, in the aggregate to the sum of six hundred and sixty-seven million, six hundred and fifty-seven thousand, one hundred and fifty pounds.

##### 3 Short title.

**3.**—This Act may be cited as the Appropriation Act, 1988.
